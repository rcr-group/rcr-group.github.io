Reversible Computing Research Group

Group

The RCR group aims at coordinating Italian research in the field of reversible computing. Reversible computing is an emerging programming paradigm which allows programs to execute not only in the standard, forward direction, but also backwards, undoing actions and recovering past states. Reversible computing is attracting interest due to its many applications in areas as different as simulation, robotics, debugging, biochemical modeling, quantum computing, and many others.
